# Change Management

## Meridian Housing Group - User Adoption Strategy

*Adoption approach ensuring sustainable value from the Building Safety Platform investment.*

---

## Overview

Technology only delivers value when people use it effectively. This change management plan ensures Meridian staff, residents, and partners adopt the Building Safety Platform and realise the expected benefits.

**Change Principles:**
- People before technology
- Start with why (Building Safety Act compliance)
- Champions lead adoption
- Continuous engagement, not big bang training
- Measure and iterate

## Resistance Management

### Anticipated Resistance

| Source | Resistance | Root Cause | Mitigation |
|--------|------------|------------|------------|
| **Long-tenured staff** | "Old system works fine" | Comfort, fear of change | Involve early, show problems with current |
| **Spreadsheet owners** | "My spreadsheet has more detail" | Loss of control | Migration support, show benefits |
| **Surveyors** | "Technology slows me down" | Device unfamiliarity | Offline mode, practical training |
| **Housing Officers** | "Not my job" | Workload concern | Show time savings, limit scope |
| **Managers** | "Team won't have time" | Resource pressure | Phase approach, visible exec support |

## Go-Live Support

### Support Model

| Support Type | Description | Availability |
|--------------|-------------|--------------|
| **Floor Walking** | Champions visible in office | First 2 weeks |
| **Drop-in Sessions** | Scheduled help sessions | Daily, first week |
| **Help Desk** | Phone/Teams support | 8am-6pm, extended first month |
| **Self-Service** | FAQ, guides, videos | 24/7 |
| **Super User** | Expert assistance | Same day |

### Go-Live Checklist

- [ ] All users have accounts
- [ ] Training completed (>90%)
- [ ] Champions briefed
- [ ] Support schedule published
- [ ] Escalation path communicated
- [ ] Floor walking rota confirmed
- [ ] Quick reference cards distributed
- [ ] FAQ updated and published
- [ ] Comms sent (24 hours before)
